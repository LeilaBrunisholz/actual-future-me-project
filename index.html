Director for Disney Movies

Education

It is important to have experience and education in the field of film and movie making. One thing that would be very important to have is a bachelors degree in cinema studies. Multiple internships can also give one a start experience they need. Disney themselves provide internships for movies. While none of this is required to make a movie, in order to have a successful career, it is very important.

Schools with really good film programs are
  • University of Tampa
  • UCLA
  • University of Southern California
  • NYU
  • American Film Institute
  • Florida State
  • Columbia University
